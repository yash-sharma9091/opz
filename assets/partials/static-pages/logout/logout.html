Sign out of or remove a person from Zenbrisa

When you sign out of Zenbrisa, your data will stop syncing to your Account. The data you created while signed in will remain stored on the device by default, but any new data or changes you make in browser on your device won't sync going forward.

Basic Logout

If you are using your own private computer that no one else uses, “Basic Logout” will close the current session and allow you to automatically log back in the next time that you enter Zenbrisa website.

Public Computer Secure Logout

If you are using a public computer or if you want to make sure that you have a very secure logout on your private computer, it is always best to choose “Public Computer Secure Logout” . This will not only close your current session, but will completely disable any future automatic logins on that computer.
